About
Us

We are a diverse collection of thinkers and doers, believing in innovation we have been continually reimagining what’s possible to help us all do what we love in new ways.

Focusing currently on IoT and Blockchain, Winning Technologies believes in the power of research and ideas, we foster innovation and build next generation products out of it. And the same passion for innovation that goes into our products also applies to our practices: strengthening our commitment to leave the world better than we found it.

Project

1adda

1adda is a vision to push startups towards being enterprises.
We foster beautiful minds, budding leaders and future entrepreneurs with an integrated ecosystem, consisting of a powerful Mentor Panel, high grade infrastructural support and a well connected community interface.
The incubation centre is located in one of the most premier and centrally located areas of Delhi, Greater Kailash-1.
We also work very closely with budding student entrepreneurs at the university level, ensuring they put their efforts in the right direction.
