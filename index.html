About
Us

We are a diverse collection of thinkers and doers, believing in innovation we have been continually reimagining what’s possible to help us all do what we love in new ways.

Focusing currently on IoT and Blockchain, Winning Technologies believes in the power of research and ideas, we foster innovation and build next generation products out of it. And the same passion for innovation that goes into our products also applies to our practices: strengthening our commitment to leave the world better than we found it.

PROJECTS

Projects

1adda

1adda is a vision to push startups towards being enterprises.
We foster beautiful minds, budding leaders and future entrepreneurs with an integrated ecosystem, consisting of a powerful Mentor Panel, high grade infrastructural support and a well connected community interface.
The incubation centre is located in one of the most premier and centrally located areas of Delhi, Greater Kailash-1.
We also work very closely with budding student entrepreneurs at the university level, ensuring they put their efforts in the right direction.

shoped

We are building 'Shoped'. A platform to connect Brand, Retailer, Consumer and the Service Ecosystem. Shoped will solve the pain of brand to reach out consumers, the pain of consumer to avail post-sales services and will generate a real-time analysis of Sales patterns, consumer behaviour and product lifecycle
It happens through creating a Blockchain enabled platform to track Proof-of-Existence, Proof-of-Sale, Proof-of-Ownership, and Proof-of-Service of a product. As it is all stored in an immutable ledger, Every product is tracked from the generation, until disposal, thus creating an audit trail.

Fuelscale

FuelScale is a complete asset monitoring and smart fuel telematics system, designed for remote fuel monitoring and improving the operational efficiency of any kind of fuel consuming asset. FuelScale while tracking fuel pilferage also gives analysis for predictive maintenance and asset performance in Real-Time.

BaaS

Nikoin Blockchain as a Service Platform provides enterprise and/or developers with the Blockchain protocol layer as microservices which can be deployed on few clicks, scalable to production environment allowing them to develop, deploy and scale fast, saving time and resources. Nikoin BaaS platform is built to perform.
